About BioMIC 2025

The conference will be held for two days and will consist of plenary and symposia sessions. The plenary sessions will be presented by a number of keynote speakers. The symposia session will consist of presentations from invited speakers and presenters for each topic, and it will take place in breakout rooms. All sessions will be conducted in English.

Introduction

Held as part of the Universitas Gadjah Mada Annual Scientific Conferences (UASC) 2025 series, the 6th International Conference on Bioinformatics, Biotechnology, and Biomedical Engineering (BioMIC 2025) provides an ideal academic platform for researchers to present the latest research findings and describe emerging technologies and directions in bioinformatics, biotechnology, and biomedical engineering.

This year, five symposia will be held, Big Data for Public Health Policy symposium; Bioinformatics and Data Mining symposium; Biomedical Sciences and Engineering symposium; Biomolecular and Biotechnology symposium; and Drug Development and Nutraceutical symposium.

BioMIC 2025 is in conjunction with the Computer, Electronics, and Information Technology symposium; the 6th Geoscience and Environmental Management symposium; the 11th International Conference on Science and Technology and the 6th International Conference on Smart and Innovative Agriculture.

BioMIC 2025 receives manuscripts of original research, review article, and case report.

Publications

The accepted and presented papers will be submitted for possible inclusion to proceedings that are indexed by Scopus/CPCI/DOAJ.
